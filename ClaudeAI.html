Claude AI is an advanced language model created by Anthropic. It specializes in deep reasoning, coding, and document analysis. Its powerful file interaction allows users to upload PDFs and images, enabling Claude to summarize them, extract data, and interpret complex visual charts.

What is Claude AI?

Developed by former AI safety researchers, Claude is built to be a reliable and highly capable AI assistant. The platform relies on a family of models like Claude Haiku (fastest), Claude Sonnet (balanced frontier intelligence), and Claude Opus (highly complex tasks).
